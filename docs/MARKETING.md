# Fypod Marketing Assets

## Chrome Web Store Description

**Headline:**
Fypod: Your AI-Powered Career Accelerator for LinkedIn

**Description:**
Transform your LinkedIn job search with AI-powered analysis. Fypod is your intelligent companion that helps you land your dream job faster.

🛡️ **The Guardian**
Protect yourself from fake jobs and predatory MLM schemes. Fypod analyzes job postings for red flags and suspicious patterns, keeping your career search safe.

🎓 **The Coach**
Prepare for interviews with Google-level technical essay questions. Get instant AI feedback on your answers and improve your technical communication skills.

🌉 **The Bridge**
Identify skill gaps and get personalized course recommendations from Fydemy to bridge the gap between where you are and where you want to be.

**Key Features:**
• Auto-detection on LinkedIn job pages
• 0-100% match score analysis
• Gap analysis with skill recommendations
• Scam detection and salary estimates
• Interactive essay preparation
• History tracking (last 15 analyses)
• Multiple AI models (Pro/Elite/Vision/Flash)
• Minimalist "Piano Black" design

**How It Works:**
1. Upload your CV (PDF format)
2. Enter your Sumopod API key
3. Browse LinkedIn jobs
4. Fypod appears automatically
5. Get instant AI-powered analysis

**Privacy First:**
Your CV and data stay local. We only send anonymized data to our AI API for analysis.
