How Fypod Works

1

Upload Profile

Click the Fypod Extension icon, navigate to settings, and drop your latest PDF CV. This serves as your baseline for all future applications.

2

Analyze Job Postings

Browse a job on LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor. Open the extension and click analyze to immediately compare the job description against your CV.

3

Strategize with Ucing

Review your Match Score and Skill Gaps. Open the chat to talk to Ucing, our AI HR recruiter, to generate a tailored interview strategy and negotiate salary.
